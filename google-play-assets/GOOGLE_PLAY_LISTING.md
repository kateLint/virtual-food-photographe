## Permission Justifications

### CAMERA
This app uses the camera to scan physical restaurant menus and extract text using OCR technology. The camera permission is essential for the menu scanning feature, allowing users to quickly input menu items without manual typing.

### READ_MEDIA_IMAGES
The app saves AI-generated food images to the device photo library. Users can download their favorite images to their gallery. This permission is required for the expo-media-library package to save generated images to the user's photo collection.
